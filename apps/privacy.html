Privacy Policy

A Team Wedding  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters. A Team Wedding is designed to enhance a shared celebration — we collect only what is necessary to make the app work, and we never sell your data.

1. Who We Are

A Team Wedding is developed and operated by GT-IT Pty Ltd ("we", "us", or "our"), an IT consulting company based in Australia. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at info@gt-it.au.

2. Information We Collect

When you use A Team Wedding, we may collect the following:

  • Content you share — photos, videos, or other media you choose to upload within the app.
  • Quiz and voting responses — your answers to the couple's quiz and song vote selections.
  • Device information — basic technical details such as device type and operating system version, used solely for app compatibility and troubleshooting.
  • App usage data — anonymous usage information to help us improve the app experience.

We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifying information unless you voluntarily provide it when contacting support.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Operate and display shared content within the app for the event it was created for.
  • Aggregate and display quiz scores and song vote tallies.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.
  • Respond to support enquiries if you contact us.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties. Content you upload (photos, videos) is visible to other guests within the same event. We do not share your data with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

We may use trusted third-party services (such as cloud hosting providers) to operate the app. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for any other purpose.

5. Data Retention

Content shared within the app (photos, videos, quiz responses, votes) is retained for the duration of the event and a reasonable period afterward to allow guests to revisit shared memories. If you wish to have your content removed, please contact us at info@gt-it.au.

6. Children's Privacy

A Team Wedding is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use the app only on trusted networks.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your content or data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by ceasing use of the app.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@gt-it.au.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact:

GT-IT Pty Ltd
Email: info@gt-it.au
